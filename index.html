CEO Vision presents The CEO Cockpit

CEO.Vision allows you to visualize very large Excel spreadsheets dynamically inside a virtual world. You can select any field and dynamically graph the data in place, exploring your information in a way that simply wasn't possible before.

CEO.Vision is designed to work both on a Mac or PC, or on a WebVR enabled headmount display device. We are targeting Google Daydream and the Samsung Gear VR devices to start, but we will support virtually every head wearable device including Oculus, Vive, and Microsoft Hololens.

You can try it out today with the Desktop Browser below. Google Daydream is coming soon.
